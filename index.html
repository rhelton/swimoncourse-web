Coming Soon to the App Store

Navigate open water by feel.

SwimOnCourse turns your Apple Watch into an open water navigation guide. Set a destination before you dive in — then just swim. Subtle haptic cues steer you back on course whenever you drift, so you can focus on your stroke, not your heading.

Features

🗺️

Set & Go

Long press the map to drop a destination pin, send it to your Watch, then leave your phone on shore.

⌚

Haptic Guidance

Feel a tap pattern when you drift — silence means you're on course. Fully configurable left and right patterns.

📍

Finish Zone

A perpendicular finish line detects arrival even with GPS drift. No more missing your destination.

📊

Swim Analytics

Route map, splits, heart rate, weather conditions, and efficiency score — all in one post-swim summary.

💧

Water Ready

Water lock activates automatically. Works with screen dimmed. No looking up required.

❤️

Health Integration

Saves Open Water Swim workouts directly to Apple Health with heart rate and calorie tracking.

Requirements

  • iPhone with iOS 17 or later
  • Apple Watch Series 4 or later with watchOS 11 or later
  • Location and Health permissions
